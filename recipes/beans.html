Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Pinto Beans
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash your beans
  2. Fill Pot with water
  3. Place washed Beans in water
  4. Boil till beans are soft 45-60 mins
  5. Salt to taste
  6. Allow beans to absorb salt for 5-10 mins
  7. Serve and enjoy!
