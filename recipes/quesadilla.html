Ingredients:

  • Cheese that can melt of choice
  • Corn Tortilla
  • Salsa (optional)

Steps

  1. Warm up your tortilla.
  2. Place your cheese on one half of the tortilla.
  3. Fold your tortilla in half.
  4. Wait for the cheese to melt.
  5. Plate up your quesadilla.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
